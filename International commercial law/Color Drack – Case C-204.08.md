**Color Drack, Case C-204/08**

  

The Court held that under **Brussels I, art. 7(1)(b)** for **sale of goods**, when there are **multiple delivery locations within one Member State**, jurisdiction is in the courts of:

- The **principal place of delivery**, determined by the partiesâ€™ agreement or, if unclear, based on factual criteria such as the most significant delivery location.
    

  

If no principal place can be identified, the claimant may sue in **any delivery location** within that Member State.

This rule ensures **predictability** and limits excessive forum shopping.
