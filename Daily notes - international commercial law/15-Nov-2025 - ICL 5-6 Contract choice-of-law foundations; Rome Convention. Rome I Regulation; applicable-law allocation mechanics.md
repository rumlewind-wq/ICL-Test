### Case 1, lecture 5

Buyer from Germany entered into a contract for the sale of goods with Seller from DK.

1) How will a German court decide the law governing the contract if there is no choice of law agreement in the contract?

2) How will a Danish court decide the law governing the contract if there is no choice of law agreement in the contract?

3) Can the parties agree on the law governing the contract?

### Case 2, lecture 5

A from Germany entered into a cooperation contract on chemical research with B from France.

1) How will a German court decide the law governing the contract if there is no choice of law agreement in the contract?

2) How will a French court decide the law governing the contract if there is no choice of law agreement in the contract?

3) Can the parties agree that the contract shall be governed by Swiss law?

4) Can the parties agree that the contract shall be governed by the Unidroit principles for international commercial contracts?
