# Incoterms 2020 Overview

> Outline note for the Incoterms 2020 rules. Populate each delivery term with risk, cost, and document allocations.

## Key deliverables to fill in
- **E-terms** – EXW responsibilities.
- **F-terms** – FCA, FAS, FOB (shipment obligations, export clearance).
- **C-terms** – CPT, CIP, CFR, CIF (risk vs cost split, insurance requirements, transport control).
- **D-terms** – DAP, DPU, DDP (arrival obligations, import clearance, delivery point).
